Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026
Your data never leaves your device

SnapTax stores all your financial data — expenses, income, mileage logs, receipts, and tax information — exclusively in your phone or browser's local storage. This data is never uploaded to any server, database, or cloud service operated by SnapTax.

What we collect
• Nothing by default. Your name, business name, expenses, income, receipts, and tax estimates stay on your device only.
• Receipt scanning (optional). If you use AI receipt scanning, receipt images are sent to Anthropic's API to be read. Anthropic's privacy policy governs this data. Images are not stored by SnapTax.
• Mileage calculation (optional). If you use the Maps distance calculator, your start and end addresses are sent to OpenStreetMap's free routing service. No personal information is attached.
Security

Your data is protected by your PIN. We use a one-way hash to store your PIN — it cannot be reversed or read. If you lose your PIN, you can reset it with your security question answer. We recommend not sharing your device or browser with others.

Your rights

You can delete all your data at any time from the Reports tab. Clearing your browser's local storage also removes all SnapTax data permanently.

No ads. No tracking. No third parties.

SnapTax does not use analytics, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking. There are no cookies beyond what your browser requires to store local data.

TurboTax import

When you import a prior year TurboTax PDF, the document image is sent to Anthropic's API for reading. The extracted data is stored only on your device. SnapTax has no affiliation with TurboTax or Intuit.

Contact

Questions about privacy? This app is provided by LifePulse AI. Contact your SnapTax provider for support.

How to download your TurboTax return
1
Sign in to TurboTax
Go to turbotax.intuit.com and sign in to your account.
2
Go to Tax Home
Click "Tax Home" or "My Returns" at the top of the page.
3
Select the tax year
Choose the year you want to import (e.g. 2024, 2023). Click "View/Print return."
4
Download the PDF
Click the download icon or "Save as PDF." Save it to your phone.
5
Find Schedule C
Open the PDF and scroll to Schedule C (Profit or Loss from Business). Take a screenshot of that page.
6
Import into SnapTax
In SnapTax, go to Reports → Import Prior Year. Upload the Schedule C screenshot. AI will read it automatically.
